Hydrophilic Swell Bar

Hydrophilic Swell Bar is a high-performance sealing solution designed to prevent water leakage in construction joints. It expands on contact with water, creating a durable and watertight seal for underground structures, tunnels, precast concrete elements, and concrete-to-steel joints. Manufactured to industry standards, it offers excellent weather resistance, long-lasting durability, and reliable waterproofing performance.

PVC water Stopper

PVC Water Stops provide a reliable watertight seal in concrete construction joints, preventing water seepage while accommodating structural movement. They are widely used in dams, tunnels, basements, water tanks, and other hydraulic structures for long-lasting waterproof protection.

All types of Bearing
